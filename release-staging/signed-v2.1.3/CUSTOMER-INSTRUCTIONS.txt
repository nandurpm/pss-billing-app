INSTALLATION / UPDATE
1. Export a backup from Settings > Backup & Restore > Export All Data.
2. Download Purple-Signature-Salon-v2.1.3.apk.
3. Open the APK on the Android phone.
4. Allow installation from this source when Android asks.
5. Tap Update or Install, then Open.

BACKUP
Use Settings > Backup & Restore > Export All Data regularly.
The backup is saved in Downloads/Purple Signature/Backups.
Keep a copy in Google Drive, email, or another safe location.

IMPORTANT UPDATE RULE
DO NOT UNINSTALL THE APP DURING UPDATES.
Install the newer APK directly over the existing app. Uninstalling can remove local bills and settings.
